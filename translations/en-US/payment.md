# Payment #

> Chinese original. If the two disagree, the original is authoritative.

## Core Concepts ##

- The points obtained from a win by draw and from a win by discard must be equal.
- There is no pao (responsibility-payment) system of any kind.

## Competitive System ##

The winner's income is fixed at twice the winning score.

### Win by Draw ###

On a win by draw, the other two players each pay the winner one times the winning score, two times in total.

### Win by Discard ###

On a win by discard, if the winning score is less than 3 points, treat it as win by draw;
if it reaches 3 points or more, the uninvolved seat pays 3 points and the player dealing in pays the remaining points.

### Exhaustive Draw ###

No penalty points; every player's income and payment are zero.

## Discarder-pays System ##

### Win by Draw ###

On a win by draw, the other two players each pay the winner one times the winning score, two times in total.

### Win by Discard ###

The player dealing in pays twice the winning score.

### Exhaustive Draw ###

No penalty points; every player's income and payment are zero.

## Equal-Pay system ##

Whether win by draw or win by discard, the other two players each pay the winner one times the winning score, two times in total. An exhaustive draw has no penalty points.

## Near-Classical system ##

The dealer has no doubling; all payments are one times the winning score.
On a win, all three players open their owned sets and pay according to the total of hand scores.
The hands are determined by the decomposed melds.

### Hand Classification ###

Because this system must consider non-winners' scores, hand types are split into three classes.

#### Action Type ####

Countable only for the winner.
All Chance-category hand types are action type;
Fully Concealed Hand and All Claimed are also action type.

#### Whole-Hand Type ####

Countable only for the winner; requires the entire set to conform to its rule.

- Terminals category
- Single-Suit category

#### Feature Type ####

Countable whenever the tiles show the feature.

- All Triplets
- Value Honors
- Honors category
- Three-Suit category
- Identical-Sequences category
- Concealed-Triplets category
- Consecutive-Triplets category
- Quartets category

### Winning ###

The winner receives points from both other players and pays nothing.

#### Dealing in ####

The player dealing in cannot receive any points.

#### Uninvolved ####

The uninvolved seat receives the total hand score of that player's owned set from the one player other than the winner.

### Exhaustive draw ###

No penalty points; every player's income and payment are zero.
