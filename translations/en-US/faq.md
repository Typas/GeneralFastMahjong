## The Sun and Moon honor tiles aren't found in ordinary mahjong sets; how to substitute?

These two honor tiles can be substituted using the "South" honor tile for "Sun", and the "North" honor tile for "Moon".

## Can calls be made consecutively?

Yes, with limits. A win ends the round, so nothing can follow it.
Chow, pung and kong (claimed quartet) can only be followed by kong (concealed quartet, or added quartet); kong (concealed quartet, or added quartet) can be followed by kong (concealed quartet, or added quartet) or a win.

## Can Robbing a Kong rob a concealed quartet?

No; no winning set may rob a concealed quartet.

## After a chow, may one discard a two-gapper tile?

Yes; there is no restriction on tiles discarded after a claim.

## Does Six Gates count only the six-sided waiting set?

Yes; only the six-sided waiting set is Six Gates.
